About ASPL Fusion

Pioneering India's clean energy revolution through fusion technology

Our Story

ASPL Fusion (Agnira Sanlayan Private Limited) was founded in 2025 with a singular vision: to make fusion energy a commercial reality in India. Born from the convergence of breakthrough advances in superconducting magnets, decades of fusion research, and India's urgent need for clean baseload power, we represent a new generation of energy companies focused on practical solutions to existential challenges.

The name "Agnira Sanlayan" combines Sanskrit words meaning "fire" (Agni) and "fusion/synthesis" (Sanlayan), reflecting both our technological focus and our deep roots in Indian culture and values. We believe that India, with its rich scientific heritage and ambitious energy goals, is uniquely positioned to lead the global fusion revolution.

Message from Leadership

Dr. Amrita Sharma

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

"India stands at a pivotal moment. Our economy is growing faster than any major nation, yet we remain dependent on imported fossil fuels that drain our resources and pollute our air. Fusion energy is not just a scientific achievement – it's an economic and environmental imperative for our nation. At ASPL Fusion, we're taking a different approach. Rather than pursuing fusion power in isolation, we're building a sustainable business that solves critical problems at every stage. From providing life-saving medical isotopes to decarbonizing heavy industry, each phase of our journey creates real value while advancing toward abundant clean energy. This is more than a company – it's a mission to secure India's energy sovereignty and demonstrate that breakthrough innovation can emerge from our nation. Together with our partners, investors, and team members, we're writing the next chapter of India's energy story."

Our Journey So Far

January 2025

Company Founded

ASPL Fusion incorporated as Agnira Sanlayan Pvt. Ltd. with seed funding from strategic angel investors.

March 2025

Core Team Assembled

Recruited world-class founding team including plasma physicists, nuclear engineers, and business leaders.

June 2025

Technology Validation

Completed preliminary design of magnetic mirror system and validated key physics parameters through simulation.

August 2025

Series A Funding

Secured $20M Series A funding to build India's first commercial medical isotope production facility.

September 2025

BARC Partnership

Signed MoU with Bhabha Atomic Research Centre for collaborative research on isotope production technologies.

October 2025

Facility Groundbreaking

Commenced construction of Phase 1 medical isotope production facility in Haryana.
