Applications

Transforming industries through fusion technology

Versatile Technology, Multiple Markets

ASPL Fusion's magnetic mirror technology platform is designed to serve multiple high-value applications. Our phased approach allows us to enter profitable markets early while progressively advancing toward our ultimate goal of abundant clean energy.

Medical Isotope Production

Phase 1 • 2025-2028

Our accelerator-based neutron sources will produce critical medical isotopes including:

  • Molybdenum-99: Parent isotope for Technetium-99m, used in 80% of nuclear medicine procedures
  • Iodine-131: Treatment for thyroid cancer and hyperthyroidism
  • Lutetium-177: Emerging therapy for neuroendocrine tumors

Benefits

  • Secure domestic supply chain
  • Stable pricing for healthcare providers
  • No weapons-grade uranium required
  • Reduced radioactive waste

Industrial Process Heat

Phase 2 • 2028-2031

Clean, high-temperature heat for hard-to-decarbonize industries:

  • Steel Production: Replace coal in blast furnaces
  • Cement Manufacturing: Decarbonize clinker production
  • Chemical Processing: Steam and heat for reactions
  • Refining: Clean heat for distillation processes

Benefits

  • Temperatures up to 800°C
  • Zero carbon emissions
  • 24/7 reliable operation
  • Reduced operating costs

Green Hydrogen Production

Phase 3 • 2031-2034

High-efficiency hydrogen generation through advanced electrolysis:

  • Transportation Fuel: Heavy trucks, ships, and aircraft
  • Ammonia Production: Fertilizer manufacturing
  • Steel Making: Direct reduction of iron ore
  • Energy Storage: Grid-scale storage solution

Benefits

  • 50% higher efficiency than conventional electrolysis
  • Competitive with grey hydrogen costs
  • Supports national hydrogen mission
  • Enables export opportunities

Grid-Scale Power Generation

Phase 4 • 2034-2040

Clean, reliable baseload electricity for India's growing needs:

  • Utility Scale: 400-1000 MW power plants
  • Industrial Parks: Dedicated clean power supply
  • Data Centers: 24/7 carbon-free electricity
  • Grid Stability: Complement variable renewables

Benefits

  • Zero carbon emissions
  • No long-lived radioactive waste
  • Abundant domestic fuel supply
  • Small land footprint
