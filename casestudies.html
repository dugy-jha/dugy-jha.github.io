Our strategy is architected to solve critical national challenges at every phase, creating immense value long before our first fusion power plant comes online.

At ASPL Fusion, we believe in practical innovation. Our phased approach is designed to create meaningful impact at every step of the journey while methodically de-risking our path to commercial fusion energy. Each phase addresses a critical national need while advancing our core fusion technology.

Case Study 1: Securing India's Healthcare Future

Healthcare & Commercial Viability | Phase 1

The Challenge: A Critical National Vulnerability

The global supply of Molybdenum-99 (99Mo), the parent isotope for the most widely used medical diagnostic imaging agent, is fragile and depends on a few aging foreign nuclear reactors. India is almost entirely import-dependent, exposing our healthcare system to price volatility and supply chain disruptions that can delay critical cancer diagnoses for millions of patients.

Our Solution: Domestic Production & Market Leadership

ASPL's Phase 1 directly confronts this challenge by building a state-of-the-art accelerator-based neutron source facility. This facility will use proven, commercially available technology to irradiate Low Enriched Uranium (LEU) targets, producing a reliable, high-purity domestic supply of 99Mo. Our go-to-market strategy targets India's nuclear medicine departments with a superior value proposition: supply reliability, stable pricing, and simplified logistics.

Key Features of Our Phase 1 Solution:

  • High Reliability: Accelerator-based system eliminates dependence on aging research reactors
  • Domestic Production: Located within India, eliminating cross-border logistics and import delays
  • Scalable Design: Production capacity can be expanded to meet growing demand
  • Quick Deployment: Uses commercially proven technology for rapid implementation
  • Revenue Generating: Creates sustainable business while funding fusion R&D

The Impact: Profitability with National Purpose

By capturing up to 80% of the domestic market, Phase 1 is projected to achieve corporate profitability in under two years. This is not just a commercial success; it's a strategic national victory. The revenue generated will fund the core R&D for our fusion technology, demonstrating our team's execution capability while solving a pressing national healthcare crisis.

Case Study 2: Building Strategic National Assets

National Security & Energy Independence | Phase 2

The Challenge: Securing the Fuel Cycle for an Energy-Independent Future

A truly sovereign energy future requires control over the entire fuel cycle. For advanced fusion and nuclear programs, this means securing a domestic supply of critical materials like Tritium and efficiently utilizing national resources like Thorium. These are complex, long-term challenges of immense strategic importance.

Our Solution: The Advanced Magnetic Mirror as a Strategic Tool

The intense neutron flux from our Phase 2 Advanced Magnetic Mirror device is a powerful and versatile tool. By surrounding the device with specialized "blanket" modules, we can execute multiple strategic missions:

Strategic Applications of Our Phase 2 Technology:

  • Tritium Breeding: By irradiating lithium blankets, we will produce our own Tritium, a key fusion fuel. This ends foreign dependence and positions India as a potential strategic supplier.
  • Thorium-to-Uranium Breeding: By irradiating Thorium-232 blankets, we will breed Uranium-233. This directly supports and accelerates India's indigenous three-stage nuclear power programme, a cornerstone of its long-term energy security strategy.
  • Clean Power Generation: A subcritical fusion-fission hybrid system capable of generating 50-100 MW of electricity as an early demonstration platform.
