Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invest in ASPL Fusion?

We welcome discussions with strategic investors who share our vision for fusion energy in India. Please contact our investor relations team at investors@asplfusion.com for more information about current investment rounds and opportunities.

Does ASPL Fusion offer internships or student research opportunities?

Yes, we offer limited internships and research positions for advanced graduate students in plasma physics, nuclear engineering, and related fields. Please visit our careers page for current opportunities or email your CV to careers@asplfusion.com.

How can I partner with ASPL Fusion for my business?

We're actively seeking partnerships with organizations in energy, healthcare, manufacturing, and other sectors that could benefit from our technology. Please fill out our contact form specifying your interest in partnership opportunities, and our business development team will reach out to discuss potential collaboration.

Can I schedule a tour of your facilities?

Due to the sensitive nature of our technology and safety protocols, facility tours are limited to qualified investors, partners, and academic collaborators. Please contact us with your specific interest, and we'll be happy to discuss possibilities.
