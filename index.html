Our Approach

A Phased Path to Impact

We follow a disciplined two-phase strategy — generating near-term value through medical applications while advancing toward grid-scale fusion energy.

Phase 1 — Near Term

Medical Isotopes & Cancer Therapy

India depends almost entirely on imports for critical medical isotopes used in cancer diagnostics. We are building domestic production capability using advanced neutron sources, paired with next-generation Boron Neutron Capture Therapy (BNCT) — a precision cancer treatment with minimal side effects.

Phase 2 — Long Term

Clean Fusion Energy

Revenue and operational expertise from Phase 1 fund the development of a compact fusion device capable of contributing to India's energy grid. Our approach provides a commercially de-risked pathway to fusion power — not dependent on a single breakthrough, but built step by step.

What We Do

Three Pillars of Impact

Our work sits at the intersection of medicine, energy, and deep technology — each reinforcing the other.

Healthcare Sovereignty

Securing India's supply of medical isotopes for cancer diagnostics and developing BNCT — a targeted radiation therapy that treats tumours at the cellular level while sparing healthy tissue.

Clean Energy

Working toward compact fusion energy systems that produce no carbon emissions, no long-lived radioactive waste, and use abundant fuel — contributing to India's climate goals and energy independence.

Deep Technology

Building proprietary simulation tools, nuclear knowledge systems, and advanced materials expertise — creating intellectual property with applications across nuclear science, industrial inspection, and beyond.

Our Technology

Built on Proven Physics

Our technology platform draws on decades of plasma physics research, adapted for near-term commercial applications.

Neutron Source Design

Advanced moderator and reflector assemblies optimised through Monte Carlo simulation for medical-grade neutron beams.

Modular Architecture

Linear device geometry enables modular construction, incremental upgrades, and reduced build times compared to toroidal systems.

Computational Tools

Proprietary simulation and nuclear knowledge graph platforms that accelerate R&D and serve the broader nuclear science community.

Safety by Design

Inherently safe fusion processes with no risk of meltdown, no weapons-grade materials, and minimal waste — designed for regulatory confidence.
