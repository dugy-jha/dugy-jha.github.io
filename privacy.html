Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Agnira Sanlayan Pvt. Ltd. (trading as ASPL Fusion, "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit asplfusion.com ("the Site") or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

When you use our contact form, you voluntarily provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

1.2 Information collected automatically

If you accept our cookie consent banner, we collect basic session data to improve your experience:

DataPurposeStorage
Visit countUnderstand return visitorsFirst-party cookie (1 year)
First visit timestampMeasure engagement over timeFirst-party cookie (1 year)
Page referrerUnderstand how visitors find usSent with contact form only
Timezone, language, screen sizeImprove site experienceSent with contact form only
UTM parametersMeasure campaign effectivenessSent with contact form only

If you decline cookies, no tracking data is collected or stored.

1.3 Information from third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google reCAPTCHA v3 — protects our contact form from spam. Google may collect hardware and software information. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries submitted through the contact form
  • Improve the Site's content and user experience
  • Protect the Site from spam and abuse (via reCAPTCHA)
  • Understand how visitors find and use our Site

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers — Formspree (form processing) and Google (reCAPTCHA), as described above, who process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal obligations — if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Cookies

We use only first-party cookies and only if you explicitly accept them via our cookie banner. We set:

CookiePurposeDuration
aspl_consentRecords your cookie preference1 year
aspl_visitsVisit counter (consent-gated)1 year
aspl_first_visitFirst visit date (consent-gated)1 year

We do not use any third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics platforms (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).

5. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions — retained for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, then deleted within 12 months.
  • Cookies — expire after 1 year or when you clear your browser data.

6. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • reCAPTCHA v3 spam protection
  • Honeypot fields to prevent bot submissions
  • No storage of sensitive financial or health data

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent — decline cookies at any time by clearing your browser cookies; the consent banner will reappear on your next visit
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@asplfusion.com.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to external websites (LinkedIn, Instagram, partner sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
