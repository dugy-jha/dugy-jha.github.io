Our Team

Meet the pioneers advancing fusion energy in India

World-Class Expertise

ASPL Fusion brings together leading experts in plasma physics, nuclear engineering, materials science, and business leadership. Our team combines decades of experience from top global fusion research programs with deep understanding of India's energy landscape.

Leadership Team

Dr. Amrita Sharma

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Previously led fusion research at ITER, with 15+ years in plasma physics and fusion engineering. PhD from IIT Bombay and postdoctoral research at MIT Plasma Science and Fusion Center.

Dr. Rajiv Mehta

Chief Technology Officer

Expert in plasma confinement systems with 20+ years of experience at Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory and IPR Gandhinagar. Holds 7 patents in fusion-related technologies.

Vikram Patel

Chief Operating Officer

Former executive at NTPC and Reliance Energy with deep expertise in energy infrastructure development. MBA from IIM Ahmedabad and B.Tech from NIT Trichy.

Dr. Priya Narang

Chief Scientific Officer

Specialized in nuclear materials and radiation effects. Previously headed materials research at BARC with expertise in neutron radiation environments and medical isotope production.

Technical Team

Dr. Anand Kumar

Shreya Desai

Dr. Mohammed Siddiqui

Arundhati Sen
